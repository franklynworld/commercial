Our Story

Since 2010

Coffee Haven was founded with passion for exceptional coffee and a commitment to sustainability. Our mission is to source the highest quality beans while supporting ethical farming practices.

Our master roaster carefully profiles each batch to highlight the unique characteristics of the beans, creating a cup that's as unique as its origin.

We believe coffee is more than just a beverage - it's a ritual, a moment of pause in our busy lives, and we're honored to be part of your daily routine.
